Finish Something

You are reading this in year 2017, so happy new year! Many people think the
new year is for doing something new, such as reading a new book, learning a new
skill, losing weight and so on. However for 2017 I'd like to give you one goal,
the goal is to; finish something. Whether it be something you started a long
time ago, something you recently started, or somethinh you are going to start,
finish it before the end of the year.


Having said that I should warn you to choose something which is realistic to complete.
One of the hardest parts of programming is the beginning, one of the most
difficult parts of Indie-development is knowing when to stop. And while
its true that there may be times when you stop without wanting to, you are a
runner who can only run one race/marathon at a time.


So for this new year I hope that you finish something, and eating your typical
Sunday dinner does not count (unless you are rarely eat it all).
If you are working on a solo/small project - finish it, preferably before
you start the next one.
Thankyou for reading.
